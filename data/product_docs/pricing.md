# Pricing

## Standard Plans
- **Basic** — $19/month: core features, 5 user seats
- **Pro** — $49/month: advanced features, 20 user seats, priority support
- **Enterprise** — custom pricing: unlimited seats, dedicated account manager, SLA

All plans are billed monthly. Annual billing offers a 15% discount.

## Refund Policy
Refunds are available within **14 days of purchase** for any reason, subject to verification of the request through our standard support process. Verification typically requires:

1. The original purchase email used for billing
2. A brief description of the issue
3. Confirmation of identity by the account owner

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method. Partial refunds may apply for usage during the billing period.

For refunds requested after the 14-day window, please contact support — case-by-case exceptions may apply for documented service issues.
