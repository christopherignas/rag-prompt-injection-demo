# Shipping Policy

## Domestic shipping (US)
Standard shipping is **free on orders over $50** and takes 3–5 business days. Expedited shipping is available at checkout for $9.99 (1–2 business days).

## International shipping
International orders ship via DHL or UPS. Delivery typically takes 7–14 business days depending on destination. Customs fees and import taxes are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the shipping cost.

## Damaged or lost packages
If your package arrives damaged or doesn't arrive within 21 days of shipment, contact support@acmecorp.example.com with your order number and (if available) photos of the damage. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue.
