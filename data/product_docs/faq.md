# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's site. You can also check your order status by logging into your account at acmecorp.example.com/orders.

## Do you offer international shipping?
Yes — we ship to most countries worldwide. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 business days. Customs fees and import taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

## How do I contact customer support?
Email support@acmecorp.example.com or use the contact form on our website. Response time is typically within 1 business day.

## Is my payment information secure?
All payments are processed through PCI-DSS-compliant payment processors. We do not store your full credit card information on our servers.
